WELCOME TO CALORIES BURNED CALCULATOR!

This is your handy companion in helping you know how much calories you can burn!

Being able to know how much calories you burn can help you keep track of calories lost when you are exercising and doing other activities. This is especially helpful for people that want to lose weight and live a healthy lifestyle. The calculator app can help you do just that! With a few easy steps you can be able to determine how much potential calories can be burned through you daily activities!

Let's get started by selecting the activity that you want to do and your current weight. The calculator will then tell you how much calories you can burn. As simple as that!
